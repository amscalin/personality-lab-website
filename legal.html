Refund Policy

We have a strict no-refund policy on all purchases. This isn't a money-grab — it's a commitment policy. Here's exactly how it works and why.

The Short Version

All Sales Final.

The Blueprint, Group Program, and Full Transformation are non-refundable. When you purchase, you receive immediate access to our proprietary methodology and intellectual property. Because of the digital nature of our products and the work involved in delivering coaching services, refunds are not available for any reason — including change of mind, dissatisfaction with format, lack of implementation, or perceived lack of results.

1.Why No Refunds

Our refund policy is intentional, not opportunistic. Here's the reasoning:

  • Digital intellectual property: The Blueprint contains the same proprietary methodology we use with our coaching clients. The moment you purchase, that methodology is in your hands. We can't take it back.
  • Coaching delivery: Group Program and Full Transformation involve real-time work — calls, feedback, materials, and our team's attention. That work is delivered as the program runs and cannot be returned.
  • Selection over volume: We work with a small number of people each cohort. Every spot we hold for someone is a spot we're not offering to someone else who's all-in.
  • Commitment dynamics: Personal brand work requires consistent execution over weeks and months. People who can refund tend to give up at the first hard moment. We'd rather you commit fully or not at all.

By completing a purchase, you confirm that you understand and agree to all of the following:

  • You are paying for access to our methodology and the services described — not for guaranteed outcomes
  • Your results depend entirely on your effort, consistency, and execution

  • You will not request a refund, dispute the charge with your bank, or initiate a chargeback for any reason covered by this policy
  • If you initiate an unauthorized chargeback, we reserve the right to revoke access, pursue collection, and pursue legal action for the disputed amount plus costs
Before You Buy

If you are not 100% certain you want this, please do not purchase. Read the program page. Email us if you have questions. Take the quiz. Follow our content for a few weeks. We'd rather you wait until you're ready than enter half-in and feel disappointed.
